INTRODUCTION

Air University is a professional military education university system of the United States Air Force. It is accredited
by the Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schoolsto award master's degrees.
The Air University (AU) is a federally chartered public sector research university in Pakistan. Established in 2002,
its main campus is located in the capital city of Islamabad, Pakistan. The university has two other functional
campuses, Aerospace and Aviation Campus at Kamra and a campus at Multan.
